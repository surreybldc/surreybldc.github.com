Some common questions

Beginners often ask us similar things, so see if your question is somewhere below before you email us.

Money questions

Can I try before I buy?

Definitely! The first lesson for anyone that's not been before (to our society lessons) is free, so you can see what we do and consider whether or not you'd like to come again.

Do I need to purchase membership?

We always recommend you do - you can receive deals that make classes work out cheaper for you. If you are competing you must be a member as the price includes insurance.

Class related questions

What is the beginners' class?

The beginner classes are aimed at absolute beginners and will gradually work through various dances with progression through the year.

And what's accelerated beginners?

The accelerated beginners class runs at a faster rate for dancers with some previous experience, or for anyone who's particularly eager to learn.

Eligibility questions

I'm not a (Surrey) student, can I still dance?

As a student club part funded by the Student's Union our priority is to offer dancing to students, but do ask - membership for non-students is at the discretion of the committee.

What standard do I have to be?

Any! We cater for complete beginners with no coordination, to the very advanced. Our teachers can teach you from any level, to any level.

What if I'm struggling?

Everyone struggles at the beginning, and even after the going gets tough sometimes. The best advice to you if you really want to dance is to STICK AT IT - it takes time, but in the long run you'll have the confidence to dance to almost anything!

Other questions

What dance styles are there?

We offer Ballroom, Latin American, and (depending on participation) Rock n' Roll and Offbeat as extras.

What are the medals?

At the end of the year you are able to take an ISTD medallist exam which will give you a more permanent memento of your achievements with us that you can put on your CV. Contact us for more information.

I've got a question not answered here

Then drop us an email or find us on Facebook, and we'll see if we can answer it. If we don't know the answer ourselves, we'll pass it along to someone who does.
